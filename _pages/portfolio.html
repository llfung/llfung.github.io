--- layout: archive title: "Research" permalink: /portfolio/ author_profile: true --- {% include base_path %} I am a fluid dynamicists with a broad intereset in biological fluid dynamics, suspension dynamics, mixing and transport and pattern formation. I am particularly interested in the modelling of swimming microorganisms and their transport in complex flow environments such as the ocean. They present a unique challenge from a modelling perspective as they are often small enough to be considered Stokesian, but live inside high Reynolds number flow. I am also interested in the modelling of their collective behaviour and the emergence of large scale patterns due to their collective forcing on the large-scale flow. I use a combination of analytical and numerical techniques to study these problems. Below listed some of my past and current research.
