Cohort-sized tutorial class for the Fundamental of Fluid Mechanics module in the MSc programmes, tailored for MSc students with no background in fluid mechanics.

Syllabus
======
* Conservation of mass and momentum
* Incompressible and irrotational flow
  * Potential flow
* Lift and Drag
  * Circulation
* Laminar and turbulent flow

Teaching Format
======
Interactive, big-class tutorial session to go through problem sheets set by lecturer, with highlights on skills and techniques required to tackle problems in problem sheets and exams.
