Small class tutorial for the second year fluid dynamics module in the UG programme in applied mathematics.

Syllabus
======
* Conservation of mass and momentum
* Incompressible and irrotational flow
  * Potential flow
* Viscous and inviscid flow
* Parallel flow
* Wave equation

Teaching Format
======
Small-class tutorial to go through problem sheets set by lecturer, with in-depth discussion on problems students may struggle with, feedback on their misconceptions and easy mistakes.

Dates
=====
From 2022 till now.
