Cohort-sized tutorial class for second year Mathematics in the MEng Aeronautical Engineering programme.

Syllabus
======
* Vector Calculus
  * Divergence, Curl and Gradient
  * Line, surface and volume integral
  * Spherical and Cylindrical Coordinates
  * Divergence, Green's , Gauss' and Stokes' theorem
* Matrix
  * Basic matrix operations
  * Determinants
  * Eigenvalues and eigenvectors

Teaching Format
======
Interactive, big-class tutorial session to go through problem sheets set by lecturer, with highlights on skills and techniques required to tackle problems in problem sheets and exams.
